Cinematic Intro — Spotlight Search → Overhead Cone

Dark stage. A searchlight glides across looking for the title. At the end, an overhead cone of light descends — narrow at the top, flaring out below to spotlight both words while the sides of the stage stay dark. Words stay fully black during the search, fade to white as the cone locks in. 930×500 stage matches the module canvas width.

Scribble Wipe — Round-Select Logo

Boomerang loop: erase → pause → fill back in → pause → repeat. The outline (2px white, transparent fill) sits underneath the full-white fill. The sprite mask only affects the fill layer, so the outline is exposed as the fill dissolves and re-covered as the fill redraws. Intended as the module's animated logo on the round-type picker card.
